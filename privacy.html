Privacy Policy for Flux On

Last updated: October 26, 2025

Flux On ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our email automation service.

1. Information We Collect

When you choose to connect your Google Account to Flux On, we adhere to the OAuth 2.0 protocol to access specific data. We only collect:

  • Google User Profile: Your name and email address (to identify your account).
  • OAuth Tokens: Access and Refresh tokens required to send emails on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data strictly to provide the services you requested:

  • To authenticate your identity.
  • To send automated email campaigns that you compose and schedule via our dashboard.
  • To track the status of sent messages (e.g., successful delivery).

3. Google API Services User Data Policy

Flux On's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your OAuth tokens are transmitted securely via HTTPS. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your tokens against unauthorized access. We do not store your Google account password.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Your data is only shared with our internal automation infrastructure (n8n) strictly for the purpose of executing the email campaigns you initiate.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke Flux On's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting your Google Account Security Settings.
