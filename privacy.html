Privacy Policy for Flux On

Last updated: October 26, 2025

Flux On ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and operating with full transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use Flux On’s email automation platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you choose to connect your Google Account to Flux On, authentication is handled securely using Google OAuth 2.0. We only access the minimum data required to provide our services.

  • Google User Profile Information: Your name and email address to identify and manage your Flux On account.
  • OAuth Tokens: Secure access and refresh tokens issued by Google to enable email sending and automation on your behalf.

Flux On may request limited access to the Gmail API, including permission to send emails and access necessary message metadata strictly for campaign delivery and automation purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to deliver the functionality you explicitly enable:

  • To authenticate and verify your identity.
  • To send automated email campaigns that you create, schedule, and approve within the Flux On dashboard.
  • To monitor delivery status and system performance related to your campaigns.

3. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Flux On’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs strictly adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

We do not use Google user data for advertising, resale, or any purpose beyond the services you explicitly authorize.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data transmissions occur over secure HTTPS connections. OAuth tokens are stored securely using industry-standard security practices designed to prevent unauthorized access.

Flux On does not store your Google account password at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is only shared internally with our automation infrastructure (including n8n workflows) for the sole purpose of executing the email actions you initiate.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

OAuth tokens and related account data are retained only for as long as necessary to provide the requested services. You may request deletion of your data or revoke access at any time, after which all associated tokens will be invalidated.
